ABOUT US

Welcome to Evening Light Tabernacle

Evening Light Tabernacle is a vibrant community of believers dedicated to spreading the light of God's word. We are committed to serving our community and helping individuals grow in their faith journey.

Our Mission

Our mission is to be a beacon of hope and faith in our community, guided by the timeless truth of Scripture: "Thy word is a lamp unto my feet, and a light unto my path" (Psalm 119:105).

Our Values

We believe in worship, fellowship, discipleship, ministry, and evangelism. Through these pillars, we strive to create a welcoming environment where everyone can experience God's love and grace.

Join Us

We invite you to join us for worship services, Bible studies, and community events. Whether you're new to faith or have been walking with God for years, you'll find a home here at Evening Light Tabernacle.
